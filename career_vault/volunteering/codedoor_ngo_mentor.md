# CodeDoor — Volunteering

## Verbatim

Extracurriculars / Volunteering: Since moving to Munich, I've been volunteering at the "CodeDoor" NGO. Since mid-2023, I spend about one Saturday a month mentoring refugees and helping them learn basic web development (HTML/CSS/JS) so they can find junior jobs. It's really rewarding and definitely improves my empathy and teaching skills.
