# FinPayTech — Junior Software Developer → Mid-level Data Engineer (Berlin)

## Verbatim

Before CloudScale, I lived in Berlin and worked at a mid-sized startup called FinPayTech from October 2020 until February 2023. I started as a Junior Software Developer and got promoted to Mid-level Data Engineer in 2022. In the beginning, I was just writing endless SQL queries (PostgreSQL) and building Tableau dashboards for the marketing team. But then I got involved in the core backend team. I helped refactor their legacy PHP backend into Node.js microservices. I wrote hundreds of RESTful APIs. One cool thing I did was implementing a Redis caching layer that sped up the app's login time by 2 seconds, which drastically reduced the bounce rate on the app. I also introduced GitHub Actions for our CI/CD pipeline because before that, people were manually deploying code, which was a nightmare. I organized weekly "Lunch & Learn" sessions for the tech team to share knowledge.
