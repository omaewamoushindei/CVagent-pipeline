# Robotics Club — Vice President (METU)

## Verbatim

For my Bachelor's, I studied Computer Engineering at Middle East Technical University (METU) in Ankara, Turkey, from September 2014 to June 2018. I was super active there. I was the Vice President of the Robotics Club for two years, managing a team of 20 students to build autonomous line-following robots. We actually won 2nd place in a national robotics competition in 2017.
