# MenuSync — SaaS Startup Attempt (2021–2022)

## Verbatim

What about side projects? Oh boy. In 2021, a friend and I tried to launch a SaaS startup called "MenuSync". The idea was to help restaurants update their menus across UberEats, Wolt, and their own website from one dashboard. I was the sole technical founder. I built the whole frontend in React and Next.js, and the backend with Node.js and Firebase. We integrated the Stripe API for payments. We got about 50 beta testing restaurants, but we couldn't figure out the sales part, so we shut it down in late 2022. Still, I learned a MASSIVE amount about full-stack development and product management.
