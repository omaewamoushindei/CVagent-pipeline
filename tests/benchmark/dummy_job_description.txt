For our Data & Architecture division within the Analytics Tribe, we are looking for a Data Engineer (m/f/x) to join our team in Berlin or Frankfurt, either full-time or part-time (minimum 30 hours per week). You will be part of the "Data Pioneers Squad," collaborating with cross-functional colleagues across multiple locations to drive our data infrastructure forward.
Your Responsibilities

    You will work in an agile environment with full-stack responsibility for the development, automation, and continuous improvement of a scalable data platform based on a hybrid cloud architecture (on-premise & public cloud).

    You will ensure the reliable operation and advancement of our on-premises data infrastructure (S3, OpenShift, Docker, Airflow) as well as design our public cloud data platform based on Databricks.

    You will build and maintain robust data pipelines for internal data consumption, focusing heavily on tenant separation, data governance, reproducibility, and high performance.

    You will collaborate closely with internal stakeholders and partner banks, supporting them directly in projects on their own Databricks environments—from initial data integration to productive operation.

    You will actively shape the platform architecture and toolchain design, particularly regarding infrastructure automation (Terraform), system monitoring, and CI/CD workflows.

    Working within an interdisciplinary team, you will create sustainable, production-ready data solutions that drive business value.

Your Profile

    You hold a degree in Computer Science, Business Informatics, Mathematics, or a related field, and bring several years of hands-on experience in data engineering.

    You possess deep technical knowledge of Python and excel in developing and orchestrating complex data pipelines using tools like Apache Airflow.

    You have practical experience navigating containerized environments (Docker, Kubernetes/OpenShift) and are highly proficient with object-based storage (e.g., S3).

    You are highly familiar with the Databricks ecosystem—including Delta Lake, Notebooks, MLflow, and Unity Catalog—and can confidently implement customized projects within these environments.

    You bring solid experience with major cloud infrastructures (Azure/AWS/Google Cloud), Infrastructure-as-Code principles (Terraform), and a strong understanding of CI/CD pipelines for automated platform operations.

    You are communicative, customer-oriented, and maintain a confident demeanor, even in consultative or client-facing scenarios.

    You display a strong willingness to learn and continuously adapt to emerging data technologies.

    You possess fluent German skills (at least C1 level) and highly proficient English skills (minimum B2 level).

    Your working style is highly structured, independent, and team-oriented.
